The Anatomy of Type

Most species have some form of communication, though the letterform is something that makes us inherently human. Type has become such a fundamental part of the human condition that we actually classify it in anatomical terms. My project explores the intrinsic relationship between humans and letterforms whilst also informing of the science that is the anatomy of type.
