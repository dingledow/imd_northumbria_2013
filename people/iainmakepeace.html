Action Trope

ActionTrope is a creative project, which is designed to achieve the maximum customer or user interface experience. By the combination of different design technologies and skills, such as Electronic control, along with attractive photographic elements, we are able to experiment with the power to create the illusion of movement. The ultimate objective of the project was to create an interesting and attractive method of displaying different landscapes, which could be manipulated in both speed and direction. The ActionTrope has the exciting advantage of being used as a display framework, or as the entertaining tool for education. The ability to display different panoramas for a variety of reasons, gives the user a vast field or scope for use.
