En-Route

En-Route is an interactive bicycle project, focused on creating an immersive cycling experience using film, displayed in front of the rider. Using sensors and touch OSC, along with Arduino and processing, I have created a bicycle that is both interactive and intuitive, as the user begins to pedal, the film playing in front of them begins to speed up. Once at full speed the rider is then able to choose which film they want to experience, using the iPod mounted on the handlebars. I am planning on taking this project further, as the concept behind this installation is to use social media to expand En-Route, by creating an app that allows users to choose a vast array of videos filmed by cyclists around the globe be it Bradley Wiggins on a training ride, or your best mate away on a cycling holiday in Italy. Sharing experiences in a physical environment.
