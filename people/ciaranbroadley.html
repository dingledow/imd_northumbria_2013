A Journey Into Outer Space

A journey into outer space is an educational booklet designed for those who are interested in learning about our solar system. It combines timeless print design with cutting edge augmented reality to portray the universe in a way that pushes the boundaries of our age of technology.

High definition imagery, 3D rendered models of planets orbiting, high quality footage of two icons of space travel and credible information about each planets gravitation pull, atmosphere and size, for example, these are just a few of the extraordinary and inspiring characteristics which make this booklet immersive and enjoyable for a wide array of people to interact with.
